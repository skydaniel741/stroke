World record

Base times sourced from World Aquatics: SCM 2025 table (valid 01 Sep 2025 – 31 Aug 2026) and LCM 2026 table (valid 01 Jan 2026 – 31 Dec 2026). Formula: P = 1000 × (B/T)³, where B is the base time and T is your time, both in seconds. World record details are long course (50m) only for now — short course records are coming soon.

Your training zones (per 100m)

CSS per 100m = (400 time − 200 time) ÷ 2. Your CSS approximates the pace you could hold for a maximal 1500m — threshold sets (like the CSS Threshold 100s in the Set Library) are swum at this pace with short rest. Retest every 6–8 weeks.
